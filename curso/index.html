Start streaming

CURSO is an advanced payment system powered by real-time streaming payments, allowing funds to be transferred by the second, directly from the sender to the receiver.

Money streaming guarantees security for both parties. When a payment stream is initiated, the funds are temporarily held in the sender’s account, giving the receiver confidence in the sender’s financial reliability.

Meanwhile, the sender has the flexibility to cancel the transaction at any time if something unexpected comes up, ensuring their funds are protected and minimizing potential losses.

One of the major advantages of streaming payments is that you can access your money immediately, without waiting for a specific payday or due date. It’s instant and convenient.

With streaming payments, trust becomes possible, even with strangers. CURSO lets you send payments with confidence — no surprises, no risks, no stress about your money. Just let it flow.

Why curso?

Sophisticated tech made simple

Although the technology of financial streaming is based on blockchain, CURSO provides an intuitive interface that makes it easy and convenient to use. We only use one cryptocurrency — USDC, a stablecoin that makes payments feel as easy as using dollars, but in crypto.

A universal payment system

Streaming payments are versatile and can be used in a variety of situations. We offer a range of streaming options to suit your specific needs. You can customize the payment process or use pre-set streams. Our team is always here to help.

A safe solution

We use a standard protocol based on the secure and reliable Polygon blockchain, as well as the well-known and trusted USDC stablecoin. This ensures that there are no risks or deception.

Decentralized finance is the future

Our goal is to make cryptocurrency and decentralized financial tools more accessible to everyone. We believe that everyone, regardless of their location, should have the right to use a reliable and modern payment system. It should easy to use for all.

With CURSO, you don’t depend on banks, verification processes, or political situations. It’s just you, CURSO, and the person you’re paying. That’s all you need to START STREAMING.
